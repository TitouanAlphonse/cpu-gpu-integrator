# Orbital parameters file

# Format :
Each line corresponds to a time-step
1st step : line 1
2nd step : line 2
...

Each line has the following format :
step    a of the 1st massive body    e of the 1st massive body      i of the 1st massive body     Omega of the 1st massive body     omega of the 1st massive body   M of the 1st massive body     ...     omega of the last test-particle       M of the last test-particle


a is given in a.u.
i, Omega, omega and M are given in degrees
